# SalonPro Feature Roadmap

This document outlines the planned trajectory for new features and enhancements for the SalonPro system, aimed at expanding its capabilities from a core billing system to a comprehensive salon management platform.

---

## 1. Core Feature Enhancements

### 1.1 Appointment Scheduling & Online Booking
**Priority: High**
*   **Client Portal**: Allow clients to book, reschedule, and cancel appointments online through a dedicated booking widget or portal.
*   **Calendar View**: A comprehensive calendar interface for salon staff to manage their schedules and view upcoming bookings.
*   **Automated Reminders**: Integration with SMS and email services to send automated appointment reminders, reducing no-show rates.

### 1.2 Client Management (CRM)
**Priority: High**
*   **Client Profiles**: Store detailed client information including contact details, service history, and product purchase history.
*   **Preferences & Notes**: Track specific preferences (e.g., favorite stylist, exact color formulas used) and private staff notes.
*   **Marketing Consent**: Manage opt-in status for promotional materials.

### 1.3 Inventory Management
**Priority: Medium**
*   **Stock Tracking**: Track quantities of retail products and professional salon supplies (e.g., color tubes, developer).
*   **Reorder Points**: Automatic alerts when stock falls below defined thresholds.
*   **Usage Deduction**: Automatically deduct professional supplies from inventory when specific services are billed.

### 1.4 Integrated Payment Processing
**Priority: Medium**
*   **Payment Gateways**: Integrate with providers like Stripe, Razorpay, or Square to accept credit/debit cards directly within the app.
*   **Contactless/Wallet Payments**: Support modern digital payment methods alongside traditional cash/card.

### 1.5 Staff Payroll Integration
**Priority: Medium**
*   **Payroll Calculation**: Expand the existing staff split system to automatically generate complete payroll reports based on commission splits, base salary, and attendance.
*   **Attendance Tracking**: Basic clock-in/clock-out functionality for staff members.

---

## 2. Advanced Features for Future Development

### 2.1 Marketing & Loyalty Programs
**Priority: Low**
*   **Campaign Management**: Send targeted SMS or Email campaigns (e.g., birthday specials, "we miss you" offers).
*   **Loyalty Points**: A points-based system rewarding frequent clients, redeemable for discounts on future services or products.

### 2.2 Advanced Reporting & Analytics
**Priority: Low**
*   **Deep Analytics**: Expand the dashboard to include reports on service popularity trends, staff retention rates, and inventory turnover.
*   **Custom Reports**: Allow administrators to build and export custom data views.

### 2.3 Multi-Location Support
**Priority: Low**
*   **Enterprise Architecture**: Adapt the database schema to support multi-tenancy for salon owners with multiple physical locations.
*   **Centralized Dashboard**: Allow owners to view aggregate data across all branches or drill down into specific locations.

### 2.4 Mobile Application (PWA / Native)
**Priority: Low**
*   **Client App**: A dedicated app for clients to book appointments and view loyalty points.
*   **Staff App**: A mobile interface for stylists to check their schedule and daily earnings on the go.

### 2.5 Gift Cards & Packages
**Priority: Low**
*   **Digital Gift Cards**: Creation, sale, and tracking of digital gift balances.
*   **Prepaid Packages**: Sell bundles of services (e.g., "Buy 5 Haircuts, Get 1 Free") and track their redemption over time.
